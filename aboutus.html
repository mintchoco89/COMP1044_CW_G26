ABOUT US



This library was founded in 2022. Since then, it has been serving the people of Selangor as a community library, which housed various reading materials, programs, and services to suit the needs of children, teenagers and adults.

This library's newsletter is published monthly to provide our fellow newsletter subscribers with the latest events going on in the community. The library also provides a decent and relaxing parking experience, as well as a coffee bar for daily doses of energy boost.

Do head to our newly-constructed lounge to make song requests so that everyone in the library can vibe along. If you're more of a silent reader, do make yourself comfortable in our silent reading zone.
